# UN Sustainable Development Goals Alignment

## Primary Alignment: SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-Being

### Target 3.8: Achieve universal health coverage

Care Sync directly supports SDG 3.8 by improving the quality and safety of healthcare delivery through:

1. **Reducing Medical Errors**: By detecting documentation divergence across clinical roles, Care Sync helps identify potential miscommunications that could lead to medical errors.

2. **Improving Care Coordination**: The system facilitates better communication between different healthcare providers (physicians, nurses, allied health) by surfacing inconsistencies in patient documentation.

3. **Enhancing Patient Safety**: Early detection of narrative drift can prevent adverse events by ensuring all care team members have consistent understanding of patient status.

4. **Supporting Evidence-Based Care**: By requiring all facts to be traceable to source documentation, Care Sync promotes evidence-based decision making.

### Target 3.d: Strengthen capacity for early warning, risk reduction and management

Care Sync contributes to early warning systems by:

1. **Real-Time Monitoring**: The system is designed to monitor a constant flow of clinical notes and flag inconsistencies as they occur, enabling early intervention.

2. **Risk Identification**: Documentation drift often precedes clinical deterioration. By detecting these patterns early, Care Sync supports proactive risk management.

3. **Systematic Approach**: The deterministic, rule-based detection provides a systematic approach to identifying potential care coordination issues.

## Secondary Alignment: SDG 9 - Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

### Target 9.5: Enhance scientific research and upgrade technological capabilities

Care Sync demonstrates innovation in healthcare technology by:

1. **Hybrid AI Approach**: Combining AI (for NLP) with deterministic code (for enterprise logic) represents an innovative approach to healthcare AI that balances capability with safety.

2. **Enterprise Integration Ready**: The system is designed to integrate with existing EHR systems (Cerner, Epic), demonstrating how new technologies can enhance existing infrastructure.

3. **Hallucination-Safe Design**: The emphasis on traceability and auditability addresses critical concerns in healthcare AI deployment, contributing to the field's technological advancement.

### Target 9.b: Support domestic technology development

The open, auditable architecture of Care Sync supports:

1. **Transparency**: All drift detection logic is code-based and reviewable, supporting transparency in healthcare AI.

2. **Local Adaptation**: The rule-based system can be adapted to local clinical practices and documentation standards.

3. **Knowledge Transfer**: The clear separation between AI and deterministic logic makes the system understandable and adaptable by local development teams.

## Implementation Impact

To measure SDG impact in production:

1. **SDG 3 Metrics**:
   - Reduction in documentation-related adverse events
   - Improvement in care team communication scores
   - Patient safety incident rates

2. **SDG 9 Metrics**:

   - Integration with existing EHR systems

## Conclusion

Care Sync aligns with UN SDGs by improving healthcare quality and safety (SDG 3) while demonstrating innovative, enterprise-ready technology (SDG 9). The system's focus on safety, auditability, and integration makes it a valuable contribution to sustainable healthcare development.
